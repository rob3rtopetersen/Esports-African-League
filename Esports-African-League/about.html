ABOUT


African E-sports League is a platform that seeks to digitalize access to gaming and offers an experience unique to the user.

Different games are offered, a user is able to select which tournament they want to participate in, on the set date, from their mobile device.

This is a platform for the everyday gamers, as well as professionals, and seeks to join those all around Africa in participating in a fun, interactive and exciting platform!
